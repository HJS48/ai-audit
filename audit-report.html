What We Found

Where Information Dies

Internal Messaging. Decisions get made in Slack, then vanish. 22 info types—handoff details, delivery issues, brief clarifications—exist for a moment then disappear into scroll. No capture = no memory.

Captured But Trapped

Call Recordings. You record calls. But recordings sit in Fireflies/Gong unsearchable by anyone who wasn't there. Client pain points, objections, commitments—locked in audio files nobody revisits.

The Team Flying Blind

Strategy needs the most, gets the least. They depend on 8 data sources to write briefs. 5 score under 50%. They're guessing at what Sales heard, what Media learned, what Creative already tried.

What Leadership Doesn't See

Ops knows when things are breaking. You don't. Bottlenecks, resource conflicts, overservicing—flagged in Slack and project tools, never surfaced to leadership until they're fires.
